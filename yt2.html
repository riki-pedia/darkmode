YouTube

*I am not trying to promote or endorse any specific content in any way, just informing*

YouTube is a free video-sharing service on the internet owned by Google.

You could post anything on YouTube, including gaming streams, tutorials, TV shows, and much more.


Streams

Streams are live recordings being taken and shown immediately to viewers. There are music streams, gaming streams, relaxation streams, and much more. But, very few YouTubers livestream, most just upload.
