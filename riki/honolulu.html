Honolulu, Hawaii

Hawaii is a well-known, well-visited, state in America. It is widely known for being an island state, having 7 inhabited islands, and, being the only island US state. In March 2022, I was in Honolulu, which is located on the island of Oahu and is also the state capital. Because of it being an island, we had to fly there. It is very expensive just being in Hawaii. The food can't be just driven. It has to be flown or yeeted onto a cargo boat.

During my stay in Honolulu, I got to experience Hawaii and the wonders in it. The beaches, the wildlife, the nature, and the constant temperatures. All the bustling streets make for one good time. With a packed tourism industry, fun is found everywhere. You could go on a hike, go to the beach, and do much, more.

Locals

There are so many tourists that you can barely pick apart the locals from tourists. The local culture is beyond understanding! The Hawaiian language that makes up of 13 letters and, to an American, sounds like gibberish. Here are some of the words and meanings for them:

  • Aloha. (uh-low-ha). This is well known for meaning both "hello" and "goodbye".
  • Mahalo. (mUH-hall-oh). This means "thank you" in Hawaiian
